# RL and Imitation — Level 3: Research Extension

中文： [README.md](./README.md)

## Positioning

This directory is the **research extension layer** for the RL and imitation learning direction. Level 3 connects course versions to research questions, papers, and deeper implementation paths — it does not promise vague "complete implementations" but clarifies research boundaries.

## Research Directions

| Direction | Research Question | Prerequisites |
|-----------|------------------|---------------|
| Sim-to-Real RL | How to transfer policies trained in simulation to real robots? | Level 1 + Level 2 policy deployment |
| Offline RL | How to learn policies from offline datasets without interaction? | Level 1 Q-learning / deep RL |
| Safe Exploration | How to ensure robot safety during learning? | Level 2 real-time control interfaces |

## Relationship to Level 1/2

- Level 1 builds policy learning algorithm intuition
- Level 2 connects policy algorithms into ROS2 / C++ real systems
- Level 3 explores more open research questions and extension boundaries
