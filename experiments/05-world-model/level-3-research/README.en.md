# World Models — Level 3: Research Extension

中文： [README.md](./README.md)

## Positioning

This directory is the **research extension layer** for the world models direction. Level 3 connects course versions to research questions, papers, and deeper implementation paths — it does not promise vague "complete implementations" but clarifies research boundaries.

## Research Directions

| Direction | Research Question | Prerequisites |
|-----------|------------------|---------------|
| Latent World Models | How to learn world dynamics in low-dimensional latent spaces? | Level 1 dynamics prediction + deep learning foundations |
| Model-based RL | How to use learned world models to accelerate policy learning? | Level 1 + RL foundations |
| Multimodal Prediction | How to integrate vision, touch, and other modalities for prediction? | Level 1 + perception foundations |

## Relationship to Level 1/2

- Level 1 builds dynamics prediction algorithm intuition
- Level 2 connects prediction models into ROS2 / C++ planning systems
- Level 3 explores more open research questions and extension boundaries
